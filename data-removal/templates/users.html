How multi-user works

  • Each user profile stores a separate name, location, and email for scan queries.
  • Scan results are tied to the active user — switching users shows only their findings.
  • Use the person icon in the top-right navbar to switch between users at any time.
  • Deleting a user permanently removes all their scan history. This cannot be undone.
